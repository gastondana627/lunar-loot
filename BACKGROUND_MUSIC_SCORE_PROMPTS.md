## üéº Music Implementation Notes

### Volume Levels (Recommended)
- **Menu Theme:** 60-70% volume
- **Level Music:** 50-60% volume (don't overpower gameplay)
- **Victory/Stingers:** 70-80% volume
- **Warning Sounds:** 75-85% volume
- **Collection Sounds:** 65-75% volume
- **Ambient Layer:** 30-40% volume

### Crossfading
- Fade between level tracks over 2-3 seconds
- Use 1-second fade for transitions
- No fade for stingers and short effects

### Looping
- All level music should loop seamlessly
- Use fade-out/fade-in if perfect loop isn't possible
- Test loops for any clicks or pops

## üìù Generation Tips for ElevenLabs

1. **Be Specific:** Include tempo, key, mood, and instruments
2. **Reference Examples:** Mention similar music/games for context
3. **Loop Requirements:** Always specify if track needs to loop
4. **Layering:** Mention if sound should work with other audio
5. **Duration:** Be clear about exact length needed
6. **Iteration:** Generate multiple versions and pick the best
7. **Test in Game:** Always test sounds in actual gameplay context
