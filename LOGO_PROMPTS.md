## Pro Tips

1. **Keep it simple** - Logo should be readable at small sizes
2. **High contrast** - Light text on dark background works best
3. **No tiny details** - They'll be lost when scaled down
4. **Test readability** - Can you read it from 10 feet away?
5. **Match your theme** - Should feel like it belongs in your game
